# Let ShunCode Learn Your Codebase

ShunCode builds deep understanding of your project to make smart, context-aware changes.

## What ShunCode understands

- **Project structure** — files, folders, and dependencies
- **Code patterns** — your style, conventions, and architecture
- **Context** — open files, recent changes, and git history

## Getting started

1. Open your project in the editor
2. Start a conversation with ShunCode
3. It automatically indexes and understands your codebase

## Pro tips

- Use `@` mentions to reference specific files or symbols
- ShunCode remembers context across your conversation
- Point it to documentation or READMEs for extra context
