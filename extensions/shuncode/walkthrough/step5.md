# You're Always in Control

ShunCode is transparent about what it does. Review, approve, or reject every change.

## How control works

- **Plan review** — See what ShunCode intends to do before it acts
- **Diff view** — Review every code change line-by-line
- **Approval flow** — Nothing happens without your confirmation
- **Undo** — Easily revert any change

## Safety features

- File modifications require explicit approval
- Terminal commands are shown before execution
- Dangerous operations are flagged and blocked
- Full history of all actions taken

## Your workflow, your rules

ShunCode adapts to you — not the other way around. Configure approval levels, auto-run rules, and more in Settings.
